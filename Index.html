"During my school days, my mother had spoken to me regarding my career.
She told me to take up something which would allow me to take care of my home as well.
She then told me that taking up architecture would offer me this comfort.
Soon after, my best friend moved into a palatial bungalow and the architect of the bungalow would tell us the process of designing the beautiful house.
From that moment on, it was pretty clear to me that I wanted to make architecture my profession.
I went on to study at BMS College in Bengaluru, then later worked under Mr. Kumar in Chennai."
